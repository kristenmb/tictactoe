THE IMPORTANCE OF RAINFORESTS

Rainforests are one of the most bio-diverse regions in the world, and are essential to the balance of life on Earth.

"Not only do they provide air, water, medicine, food, and shelter to a multitude of living beings, they are also one of our best natural defenses against climate change because of their capacity to absorb greenhouse gases from the atmosphere." 1

The world's rainforests are indespensible. You can do your part by supporting organizations and products that promote rainforest preservation and sustainability.
